Personas Assignment

Part 1: Preparation

The selected interface was a self-checkout kiosk found in Stop & Shop.

Although the sketch provides details on the interface, a description is provided. The self-checkout provides a hands on experience to purchase goods. It helps optimize the shopper experience in terms of time and control over the purchase. The shopper scans or selects and weighs the items before placing it on the weighing station. The weighing station then verifies the items before registering it on the item display. If the weights are not aligned, the kiosk locks and requires an employee to manually verify the items. Upon completion, the customer pays on the terminal.

Interview Questions

  1. Is this your first time using this kiosk at Stop & Shop?
    • If yes, why did you use it today?
    • If no, how often do you use this kiosk when you shop here? Why?
  2. Did you have any issues using the kiosk? Have you in the past?
  3. Do you think the self-checkout kiosk is preferable to the regular check-out? In what way?
  4. Do you use self-checkout options as other stores?
    • If so, would you recommend any changes to this kiosk?

Part 2: Recording Observations

User 1

Observations

  • Few items in hand; no basket or cart
  • No items that needed to be weighed individually (such as produce)
  • Check out process was fast; fit everything on weighing station
  • Bagged and paid with card
  • Headphones in; no interacting with others

Interview Summary

  1. No, have used before; only had few items today and this was faster than waiting in line
  2. Happy that there were no issues today as there have been issues in past with kiosk locking
  3. Sometimes can be preferable; especially when you have a few items
  4. Not really; primarily shop at Stop & Shop

User 2

Observations

  • Filled basket of items
  • 1 item that had to be selected and weighed on scanner
  • Fast check out process; did not fit every item on weighing station at once
  • Paid with card
  • Did not interact with others

Interview Summary

  1. No, have used before and use it when it is busy to avoid waiting
  2. No issues today but it is usually a 50/50 chance of having issues with kiosk
  3. Sometimes can be preferable; worth to take chance of using, especially when there is no line at kiosks
  4. Sometimes use at other stores; all self-checkout kiosks are similar

User 3

Observations

  • Nearly filled basket of items
  • No items that had to be weighed
  • Check out process was paused; kiosk locked up during check out process after scanning few items
  • User looked around for employee
    Turned head and body; made eye contact with employee and raised hand slightly in direction of employee
  • Spoke to employee and employee unlocked kiosk with their card

Interview Summary

  1. No, have used before; Use when they don't have a cart or if it is busy
    Feel slightly anxious waiting in line with people and having to bag items with people waiting
  2. Yes; weighing station locked today and it malfunctions pretty often
  3. It should be preferable but it isn't when it malfunctions as it wastes times, is annoying, and creates slight panic while waiting for employee
  4. Not really; think that all kiosks would have similar issues
